CX Transformation Journey

Phase 1

Set Up

Phase 1

Set Up

Establish CX governance, leadership alignment and foundational infrastructure for transformation.

  • Formalise and announce the CX Transformation Project.

  • Set up the CX Transformation Project Team.

  • Conduct CX Maturity Audit (Alt Bank inclusive).

  • Carry out a comprehensive Internal Customer Satisfaction Assessment.

  • Deliver initial CX immersion programme for ExCo.

  • Design and set up the CX Centre of Excellence (A Dedicated Command Centre).

Phase 2

Immersion

See more

Phase 2

Immersion

Embed CX culture across teams while improving service systems and integrating CX accountability.

  • All-staff CX immersion & culture alignment program.

  • Optimise the omni-channel issue resolution system and roll-out service recovery framework.

  • Integrate CX KPIs in performance reviews and appraisals.

Phase 3

Optimization

See more

Phase 3

Optimization

Leverage AI, analytics and insights to optimize journeys and proactively improve customer experience.

  • Amplify advanced AI & predictive analytics.

  • Refine CX processes using customer insights.

Phase 4

Continuous Improvement

Phase 4

Continuous Improvement

Sustain CX through governance loops, financial impact measurement and continuous learning.

  • Measure ROI from CX enhancements.

  • Embed ongoing CX learning loops.

  • Institutionalize long-term CX governance.

Service Failure Framwork

Service Failure management Framework

Turning service failures into moments that build trust.

Things won’t always go as planned - but what truly defines us is how we respond when they don’t. The Service Failure Management Framework gives us a clear, structured way to take ownership, act quickly, and recover effectively when service falls short.

It guides us on how to acknowledge issues, fix them with urgency, communicate transparently, and restore customer confidence. More importantly, it helps us go beyond quick fixes-by identifying root causes and preventing repeat occurrences.

Because every service failure is an opportunity: to learn, to improve, and to show our customers that they can trust us to make things right.

Service Disruption Framwork

Service Disruption Management Framework

Staying in control-even when things don’t go as planned.

Service disruptions happen-but how we respond is what customers remember. The OneX Service Disruption Management Framework equips us to act quickly, communicate clearly, and stay coordinated when services are impacted.

It provides a structured approach to managing disruptions from start to finish-ensuring customers are informed, supported, and never left in the dark. From early detection to resolution and recovery, it helps us reduce confusion, manage expectations, and maintain trust, even in challenging moments.

Most importantly, it brings everyone together-so we respond as one team, with clarity, speed, and purpose.

Because in moments of disruption, consistency, transparency, and ownership make all the difference.
