What is the Mandate?

The OneX mandate is a transformation of the bank’s culture, mindset, and operating model designed to treat every customer as a single, unified individual, not just an account number or transaction. It shifts the organization from fragmented service delivery to a seamless, relationship-led experience, anchored on three core pillars: One Voice,One Relationship, One team

Why this matters?

The OneX initiative redefines banking from transaction processing to meaningful relationship building, ensuring that every interaction strengthens trust and loyalty.

What is this assessment?

The Customer Experience (CX) maturity assessment evaluates how effectively CX is embedded across the organization, and defines the path toward a more consistent, insight-driven experience.

What it covers

  • Current CX maturity stage and capability level
  • Leadership perspective on existing gaps vs. desired experience
  • Priority actions to strengthen CX delivery and overall impact

Internal Customer Satisfaction

The iCSAT exercise measures how well internal teams serve each other. This report captures findings and highlights areas of strength and opportunity across the organisation.

Key Themes

  • How teams rate the quality of support they receive
  • Bottlenecks and pain points across departments
  • Recommended actions to improve internal service
